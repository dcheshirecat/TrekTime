HOW TO PLAY — INFINITE FRONTIER
🎯 YOUR MISSION
You are a Starfleet Admiral. Explore the galaxy sector by sector, recruit legendary crew from across all Trek eras, build a powerful fleet, and make first contact with alien species. Rise from Ensign to Fleet Admiral.
🗺 THE GALAXY MAP (BRIDGE TAB)
The map shows a 5×5 grid of sectors. Dark sectors are unexplored. ◉ HERE marks your position.
Tap any sector to warp there. Warping costs energy (⚡):
• Unexplored sector → costs 15 ⚡ and triggers an Encounter
• Already explored → costs 10 ⚡, no encounter
Sector colors show territory: 🔵 Federation, 🔴 Klingon, 🟢 Romulan, 🟠 Cardassian, 🌀 Anomaly, ✨ Deep Space.
⚔️ ENCOUNTERS
Every time you enter uncharted space, you face an encounter — a distress signal, hostile contact, first contact, anomaly, or derelict.
You choose one of three approaches. Each approach uses a different skill:
• 📡 Diplomacy — hailing, negotiating, first contact
• 🔬 Science — scanning, analysis, investigation
• ⚔️ Tactical — combat, evasion, rapid response
• ⚙️ Engineering — repairs, navigation, boarding
Your success chance = your crew's average skill + your ship's stats. Missions can Succeed, Partially Succeed, or Fail — you earn XP either way.
👤 CREW (CREW TAB)
Crew members are recruited through successful missions. Each officer has 6 skills: Command, Diplomacy, Science, Engineering, Medical, Tactical.
• Tap an officer to assign/unassign them to your bridge
• Your active ship has a crew capacity limit
• Assigned crew's skills are averaged for all encounter checks
• Match crew specializations to your preferred playstyle
Use the filter tabs to browse by specialization. Rare and Legendary officers have much higher stats.
🚀 FLEET (FLEET TAB)
Ships are discovered on missions. Each vessel has different stats and crew capacity — bigger ships hold more officers and boost your encounter skills.
• Shields → boosts Engineering checks
• Weapons → boosts Tactical checks
• Sensors → boosts Science checks
• Warp → speed (flavour + future features)
• Crew Capacity → max officers on bridge
Tap any ship in your fleet to set it as your active vessel. Switching ships may drop crew if your new ship has a lower capacity.
⚡ ENERGY & ₪ CREDITS
Energy is consumed when warping. If you run low, visit the LCARS tab → Admiral Profile and use "Dock at Starbase" to refill for 50 credits.
Credits are earned from successful missions. Some alien species (Ferengi, Orion) give credit bonuses on first contact.
✦ XP & RANKS
Every encounter earns XP — success earns more, but even failures teach lessons. As your XP grows you're promoted through 11 Starfleet ranks from Ensign to Fleet Admiral.
Ensign → Lt. JG → Lieutenant → Lt. Commander → Commander → Captain → Commodore → Rear Admiral → Vice Admiral → Admiral → Fleet Admiral
📡 LCARS DATABASE
Track your overall stats, view all 12 species (known and unknown), and read your full Captain's Log. Species unlock new database entries and passive bonuses when you make first contact.
